Fly Qatar QSuites (World's Best Biz Class) for 85K Points — The Finnair Route
TheGreatIndianMiles BUSINESS CLASS POINTS TRANSFER Save ₹4–10 Lakh
Qatar QSuites (fully enclosed suite, flat bed, Diptyque kits) retails for ₹5–10 lakh. HDFC Infinia transfers to Qatar at 2:1 — terrible. But transfer to Finnair at 1:1 first, then Finnair Avios = Qatar Avios (both oneworld). You get the same QSuites seat for 85K points instead of 1,70,000. HDFC quietly offers this 1:1 Finnair route but doesn't advertise it.
VALUE COMPARISON — 85,000 POINTS
METHODWHAT YOU GETVALUE/POINT
SmartBuy (default)Economy to Europe (return)₹1.00
Direct to Qatar (2:1)Needs 1,70,000 RP for QSuites₹3.00
Infinia → Finnair → Qatar ✦QSuites seat worth ₹5–10L₹5.88–₹11.76
1
Check QSuites availability — qatarairways.com → Book → "Use Avios" → Business Class. Look for dates marked "Available" (saver rates). Opens 361 days before departure. Best routes: DOH-LHR, DOH-JFK, DOH-MIA, DOH-SIN, DOH-BKK. India routes sometimes have QSuites on A350-1000 — verify on SeatGuru.
2
Don't transfer directly to Qatar — HDFC Infinia to Qatar is 2:1. You'll halve your points. Stop here and read step 3.
3
Transfer Infinia → Finnair Plus at 1:1 — SmartBuy → Airline Partners → Finnair Plus. Finnair, British Airways and Qatar all use "Avios" within the oneworld alliance. They're the same currency. Transfer time: 2–5 business days.
4
Create a Qatar Privilege Club account (free) on qatar.com. Make sure your Avios land in your Qatar account — they transfer automatically from Finnair once in the same program.
5
Book one-way, not return — QSuites one-way is 85K Avios (USA→Doha + Doha→India covered on one booking = two QSuites legs). Book the return separately in economy or cash.
6
Pay taxes in cash — expect ₹20,000–30,000 in taxes. Cannot be avoided. Still a fraction of the ₹5–10L cash price.
7
⚠ Current caution — Strait of Hormuz crisis = elevated fuel surcharges right now. If not travelling immediately, hold points. Surcharges will come down. Saver availability will re-open. Book fast when it does — don't overthink.
